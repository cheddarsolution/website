About me

I’m Sonu Suthar, a video editor and Founder of Cheddar Solution who helps creators, coaches, and brands repurpose their long-form content into high-performing short-form videos and carousels that actually get attention.

I specialize in faceless content, talking head videos, and platform-optimized edits for Instagram, YouTube Shorts, and LinkedIn — complete with hooks, captions, motion graphics, and storytelling that keeps viewers watching till the end.

I’ve worked with clients from the U.S., Singapore, and beyond, helping them grow their reach, build their brand, and turn views into value.

What sets me apart? I don’t just “edit” — I think like a creator. Every frame I cut is with intention: to stop the scroll, spark curiosity, and deliver a message with impact.

If you're ready to turn your raw footage into content that grows your audience and your business — let's talk.
